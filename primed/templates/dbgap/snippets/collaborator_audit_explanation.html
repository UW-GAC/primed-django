To be added to a dbGaP access group, a user must have linked their AnVIL account and be named as a collaborator on a dbGaP application. Access to workspaces containing data for study-consent groups that are approved for this application is controlled by adding users' AnVIL accounts to the dbGaP access group for the application.

The audit result categories are explained below.

  • Verified includes the following:
    • PI who has linked their AnVIL account and is in the dbGaP access group for this application.
    • PI who has not linked their AnVIL account and is not in the dbGaP access group for this application.
    • A collaborator who has linked their AnVIL account and is in the dbGaP access group for this application.
    • A collaborator who has not linked their AnVIL account and is not in the dbGaP access group.
  • Needs action includes the following:
    • PI who has linked their AnVIL account and is not in the dbGaP access group for this application.
    • A collaborator who has linked their AnVIL account and is not in the dbGaP access group for this application.
    • An account in the dbGaP access group for this application that is not linked to a collaborator.
  • Errors
    • A different group is in the dbGaP access group for this application.

Any errors should be reported!
