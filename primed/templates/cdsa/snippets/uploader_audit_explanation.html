To be added to a Data Affiliate Agreement uploader group, a user must have linked their AnVIL account and be named as an uploader on that agreement. Uploaders only have access to workspaces covered under their CDSA.

The audit result categories are explained below.

  • Verified includes the following:
    • A named uploader who has not linked their AnVIL account.
    • A named uploader who has linked their AnVIL account and is in the AnVIL upload group for this agreement.
    • A named uploader who has not linked their AnVIL account and is not in the AnVIL upload group.
  • Needs action includes the following:
    • A named uploader who has linked their AnVIL account and is not in the AnVIL upload group for this agreement.
    • An account in the AnVIL upload group for this agreement that is not linked to a named uploader.
  • Errors
    • A different group is in the AnVIL access group for this agreement.

Any errors should be reported!
