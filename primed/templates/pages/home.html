PRIMED AnVIL app

Data for the PRIMED consortium is stored in workspaces on the AnVIL platform. Once data have been added to the workspaces and formatted to fit the PRIMED data model, they will be shared with the Consortium and accessible to people who have approved data access via one of the PRIMED data access mechanisms. This app facilitates the creation and sharing of workspaces for PRIMED.
