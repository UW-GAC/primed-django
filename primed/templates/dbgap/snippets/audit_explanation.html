To have access to a workspace, a dbGaP application must have an approved DAR for the data in that workspace. Access to a workspace is controlled by adding or removing the access group for a dbGaP application to or from the workspace authorization domain.

The audit result categories are explained below.

  • Verified includes the following:
    • No DAR is associated with the workspace and the access group is not in the auth domain.
    • There is a DAR associated with the workspace, but it is not approved, and the group is not in the auth domain.
    • There is an approved DAR associated with the workspace and the group is in the auth domain.
  • Needs action includes the following:
    • There is an approved DAR for the workspace, but the group is not in the auth domain.
    • A DAR that used to be approved for the workspace is no longer approved, and the group is in the auth domain.
  • Errors
    • The access group is in the auth domain of the workspace for an unknown reason.

Any errors should be reported!
